Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 09/22/2024

At AdriNailsDesign, we take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us, including:

  • Name: Used for personalizing your experience within the app.
  • Email Address: Used to communicate with you regarding your account, bookings, and updates.
  • Phone Number: Used for contacting you about appointments, notifications, or important updates.
  • Banking Information: Used for processing payments related to appointments and the purchase of courses, as well as for deposits related to services rendered. We use Square as our payment gateway to securely process these payments.

2. Why We Collect Your Information

The information collected is solely for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To manage your profile and provide personalized services.
  • Appointment Booking: To schedule, modify, or cancel appointments.
  • Contact Purposes: To notify you about updates, appointments, or any other important information relevant to the services we offer.
  • Payment Processing: Your banking information is collected to securely process payments for appointments and purchases of courses, as well as for deposits related to services rendered. We use Square as our payment gateway to ensure your payments are handled securely.

3. Who We Share Your Information With

We want to assure you that:

  • Your data is not shared with anyone: We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties, organizations, or external entities.
  • Your data is only used internally: All the information we collect is used strictly for the functionality of the AdriNailsDesign app and for communication purposes to enhance your user experience.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. We take reasonable precautions to prevent unauthorized access, use, modification, or disclosure of your data. However, no system is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information at any time.
  • Request corrections if your information is inaccurate.
  • Request account deletion if you no longer wish to use our services.

If you would like to make any changes or have questions about how we handle your data, feel free to contact us at adrinailsdesign@yahoo.com.
