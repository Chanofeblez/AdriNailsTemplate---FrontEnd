Account Deletion Policy

Effective Date: 09/22/2024

At AdriNailsDesign, we respect your right to privacy and control over your personal data. If you no longer wish to use our services and would like your account and all associated information deleted, this policy outlines the steps to follow.

1. Requesting Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your account, please contact us via email. Once we receive your request, we will begin the process of removing your account and any personal information associated with it.

Email us at: adrinailsdesign@yahoo.com

2. What Will Be Deleted

When you request to delete your account, the following information will be permanently removed from our system:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email, phone number, and any other details provided during registration.
  • Booking History: Any past appointments or bookings you've made through the app.
  • Payment Information: Any saved payment methods or transaction details.
  • Reviews or Comments: Any feedback or comments you have provided within the app.

Once deleted, this information cannot be recovered. Please ensure you have saved any necessary details before proceeding with account deletion.

3. Processing Time

We strive to process account deletion requests within 5 business days. During this time, you may still receive communications related to any ongoing appointments or transactions that were in place before your request.

4. Data Retention for Legal Purposes

In certain circumstances, we may retain specific information for legal or regulatory purposes, such as financial transaction records. This will only be done where required by law and you will be notified if this applies to your data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding the deletion of your account, or if you would like to follow up on your deletion request, please contact us at:
